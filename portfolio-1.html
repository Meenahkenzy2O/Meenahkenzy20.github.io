Who I Am

I'm Kehinde Aminat — a front-end developer, coding tutor, and STEM educator with 2–5 years of experience teaching coding and computational thinking to young learners. I believe that every child, regardless of background, deserves access to the tools that will define their future.

My teaching philosophy centres on learning by doing — turning abstract concepts into hands-on projects that students can see, touch, and be proud of.

The FMCIDE Build-A-Thon is exactly the kind of initiative I've been working towards: bridging the gap between classroom theory and real-world digital skills for Nigerian youth.
